Pain Drawing Instructions

Please read carefully:

This drawing will download as an image. You can attach it to your patient intake paperwork. Your drawing will be erased when the page is refreshed or closed.

Mark the areas on your body where you feel your pain is. Include all affected areas. Mark areas of radiation. If your pain radiates, draw an arrow from where it starts to where it stops. Please extend the arrow as far as the pain travels.

If you're filling this out on a computer or tablet, use your mouse, trackpad, or finger/stylus to draw directly over the body diagram.

Use the appropriate symbol(s) listed below:

  • Ache: >>>>>
  • Numbness: =====
  • Pins & Needles: 0000
  • Burning: XXXX
  • Stabbing: /////
  • Throbbing: ~~~~~
